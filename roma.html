Han Ji-min (Korean: =7|El; born November 5, 1982) is a South Korean actress. After minor roles in All In (2003) and Dae Jang Geum (2003-2004), Han had her breakout role in revenge series Resurrection (2005). This was followed by leading roles in period dramas Capital Scandal (2007) and Yi San (2007-2008), contemporary dramas Cain and Abel (2009) and Padam Padam (2011-2012), romantic dramas Rooftop Prince (2012), Familiar Wife (2018) and The Light in Your Eyes (2019), as well as drama film Miss Baek (2018) for which she was named the Best Actress at the 55th Baeksang Arts Awards. Recently, Han starred in an omnibus-format series Our Blues (2022) and Behind Your Touch (2023).
